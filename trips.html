School Trips

I have been fortunate enough to go on some really exciting school trips,and I wanted to share them. It blows my mind that you can just sign up for so much cool stuff.

New York City — Oct., 2025

Somehow, I was lucky enough to be selected to join the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Department's Fall trip to NYC. I joined around a dozen other students, and a few faculty, and we went around the city meeting with various startups and individuals with ties to NC State. It was inspiring to get to hear the stories and advice of so many seasoned leaders. Honestly, though, my favorite parts were all the siteseeing and activities we did. It was truly just great to have fun and get to know some of my best and brightest classmates.

4th & 5th BEIP Workshops — Oct., 2024 & 2025

For the last two years, I have participated in the 4th and 5th annual Creating Solutions in the Blue Economy Workshops. These have been through the Blue Economy Innovation Program, and have been engaging ways to gain interdisciplinary knowledge and experience. I love the focus on communites—and the local impact of technologies—and have been able to connect with some amazing people.

GE-Hitachi Nuclear Campus— Oct., 2023

When I was still a part of the nuclear engineering department, I was able to attend a guided tour of GE-HItachi's main nuclear campus. We not only got to tour the office spaces, but also the plant itself. We were walked through the entire uranium refinement and fuel manufacturing process. We saw the inital powder become enriched, formed into pellets, and finally assembled into massive fuel rods. Getting this behind-the-scenes view into an industry that I'm obsessed with was incredibly rewarding.

Backpacking

I love backpacking treks. They are such an exciting and enriching experience, and I am so thankful for all the amazing oppurtunities I have had through Scouts. Spending time in the wilderness and in our natural world is so fulfilling to me, and I sincerely regret that it is not something I we do more often.
